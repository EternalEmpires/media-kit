# Eternal Empires Media Kit Guidelines

## §1 Scope of Application
1. These guidelines govern the use of all content from the official Eternal Empires Media Kit (“Media Kit Content”).
2. Usage is permitted worldwide and by anyone, provided the following rules are followed.

## §2 Principles of Use
1. Use of the Media Kit Content is allowed without prior approval, as long as it complies with these guidelines.
2. Usage is only permitted in connection with Eternal Empires.
3. Any use that may create confusion with official channels or content from Eternal Empires is strictly prohibited.

## §3 Commercial Use & Merchandising
1. Commercial use of the Media Kit Content is permitted, unless §3 (2) applies.
2. For use of Media Kit Content on physical products intended for direct sale (e.g., merchandise, clothing, stickers, etc.), written permission from Eternal Empires is required. To request written permission, please contact the Eternal Empires team at [support@eternalempires.net](mailto:support@eternalempires.net).

## §4 Permitted & Prohibited Content
1. Use of the Media Kit Content is prohibited if:
   * it violates the Eternal Empires Terms of Use or applicable law,
   * it results in pornographic, racist, extremist, offensive, or violent content,
   * it conveys political, religious, or socio-critical messages,
   * it involves NFTs, cryptocurrencies, blockchain, or AI/Machine Learning training,
   * it gives the impression that Eternal Empires is speaking, acting, or cooperating officially, when this is not the case,
   * it is used to attack, insult, or disparage other individuals, companies, or products.

## §5 Logo and Brand Use
1. When using the Eternal Empires logo, at least 51% of the visible logo area must remain unchanged.
2. Combining the logo with other brand logos is only permitted if it reflects reality (e.g., “Used by”, “Sponsored by” – only when Eternal Empires is truly involved).
3. The logo may be used for profile pictures, banners, and social media, provided there is no risk of confusion with official Eternal Empires channels.

## §6 Editing and Adaptation of Content
1. Texts from the Media Kit may only be used in full or as quotations; they may not be changed, shortened, or taken out of context.
2. Audio content from the Media Kit must remain clearly audible in the top audio track and may not be overlaid. Background music is permitted.
3. Graphics, artworks, and images may be edited or used in collages, as long as a clear reference to Eternal Empires remains.
4. Distribution or collection of Media Kit Content as a standalone archive, asset pack, or download package is not permitted.

## §7 Source Attribution and Visibility
1. The “Eternal Empires” brand must always be clearly visible in the immediate context of the used Media Kit Content.
2. If Eternal Empires content is only a small part of a work, an explicit source reference (“Source: Eternal Empires”) is required.

## §8 Advertising and Monetization
1. Use of Media Kit Content in advertising is permitted, provided all other rules in these guidelines are observed.
2. Especially, advertising with Eternal Empires content that targets or attacks other companies or individuals is strictly prohibited.

## §9 Redistribution and Third-Party Use
1. Redistribution, re-uploading, or collection of the Media Kit or its contents (e.g., as a download package or asset archive) is not permitted.
2. The use of individual elements in original creative works (such as collages) is allowed, as long as all other rules are followed.

## §10 Quality and Care
1. There is no prescribed minimum standard of quality.
2. However, users are expected to treat the content with respect and care.

## §11 Contact and Communication
1. Anyone using Eternal Empires Media Kit Content must provide a publicly reachable contact email in the immediate area of the content (e.g., YouTube profile, website, imprint, or social media bio).

## §12 Handling Violations
1. In the event of a violation of these guidelines, Eternal Empires reserves the right to:
   * contact the individual or company to resolve the issue,
   * block or remove unauthorized content,
   * take legal action if cooperation fails or the violation is serious.

## §13 Miscellaneous
1. There are no regional restrictions; these guidelines apply worldwide.
2. The Media Kit Content must not be used in a misleading way (e.g., no use of the logo on directional signs like “Restroom” or for marking unrelated objects).

Note:
For questions or uncertainty regarding these guidelines, please contact the Eternal Empires team at [support@eternalempires.net](mailto:support@eternalempires.net).
